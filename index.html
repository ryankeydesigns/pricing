GOOGLE ANALYTICS

Google Analytics allows you the information needed to help you shape the success strategy of your business, discovering things you probably never knew about visitors on your site. Whether you have an eCommerce site or an informative site, you want to understand the behavior of your visitors to deliver better results.

Google Analytics 为您提供所需的信息，帮助您制定企业的成功战略，发现您可能从未了解过的有关网站访问者的信息。 无论您拥有电子商务网站还是信息丰富的网站，您都希望了解访问者的行为以提供更好的结果。

GOOGLE MY BUSINESS

With a Google My Business Account, you get more than a business listing. Your free Business Profile lets you easily connect with customers across Google Search and Maps. You can post photos and offers to your profile to show what makes your business unique, and give customers reasons to choose you every time.

使用“Google 我的商家”帐户，您获得的不仅仅是商家信息。 免费的商家资料可让您轻松地通过 Google 搜索和地图与客户建立联系。 您可以将照片和优惠发布到您的个人资料中，以展示您的业务的独特之处，并让客户每次都有理由选择您。

GOOGLE ADWORDS

Google Ads is an online advertising platform developed by Google, where advertisers bid to display brief advertisements, service offerings, product listings, or videos to web users. It can place ads both in the results of search engines like Google Search and on non-search websites, mobile apps, and videos.

Google Ads 是由 Google 开发的在线广告平台，广告商可以在该平台上竞标向网络用户展示简短的广告、服务产品、产品列表或视频。 它既可以在 Google 搜索等搜索引擎的结果中，也可以在非搜索网站、移动应用和视频中投放广告。
